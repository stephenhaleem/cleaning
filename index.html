Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a cleaning service? +

Booking is easy! Click the "Book Now" button anywhere on our site to schedule online, call us at (403) 555-1234, or email us at info@calgarysparkle.com. We'll confirm your booking within 24 hours.

Do I need to provide cleaning supplies? +

No, our team brings all necessary cleaning supplies and equipment. We use eco-friendly products that are effective yet safe.

How long does a typical cleaning take? +

A standard cleaning of a 2-bedroom home takes 2-3 hours. Deep cleaning services may take 4-6 hours depending on the property.

Are your cleaners insured and background checked? +

Yes, all our cleaning specialists undergo thorough background checks and are fully insured for your peace of mind.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We require a 24-hour notice for cancellations to avoid a fee. For rescheduling, contact us, and we'll accommodate your new timing.

Do you offer recurring cleaning services? +

Yes, we offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly recurring cleaning with a 10-20% discount compared to one-time cleanings.
